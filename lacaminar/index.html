About La Caminar

La Caminar: Where Style Meets Substance-La Caminar transcends the conventional boundaries of a modeling show, offering a platform where individuals are not just seen but heard. It's a celebration of confidence, intellect, and the power of self-expression. Join us as we embark on this journey where style meets substance in the most captivating way possible.

Round1: Casual wear walk

The journey begins with the casual wear round, where participants showcase their unique flair and charm in relaxed attire. Beyond mere aesthetics, this round emphasizes confidence, poise, and personal style as candidates make their way down the runway.

Round2: Formal walk

As the competition heats up, selected candidates transition into formal attire for the second round. Here, sophistication and grace take center stage as participants elevate their presence and leave a lasting impression with each step.

Round3: Q&A Session

The final round is where style meets substance in the most captivating way. Finalists face a panel of judges, not only evaluated on their outward appearance but also on their ability to articulate thoughts and ideas. Through thought-provoking questions, this round delves into the personalities, beliefs, and aspirations of each participant, revealing the depth behind the dazzle.
